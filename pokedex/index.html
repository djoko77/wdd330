Instructions

  • To search a pokemon click, type the id number or the name of the Pokemon in the search bar and click the search button. Please note that there only 898 searchable Pokemon
  • The Pokemon can be added by pressing the "ADD TO MY TEAM BUTTON"
  • You can add up to three Pokemon in your party
  • More information can be seen by clicking the name of the Pokemon in your party
  • You can remove the Pokemon from your party by pressing the X button
